## Scope of third-party content in the bibles themselves

The bibles (`bibles/WEB.md`, `bibles/MOBILE.md`, `bibles/WEARABLE.md`, `bibles/XR.md`, `bibles/ICONS.md`) **do not** bundle third-party content. They:

- Cite published URLs (Apple HIG, Material 3, WCAG, NN/g, Baymard, AppTweak, SplitMetrics, Khronos, etc.)

- Never reproduce long verbatim passages from copyrighted sources
